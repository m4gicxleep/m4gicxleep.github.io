Categories

All sections of the blog. Each one is an evolving collection of notes.

  • Articles Long-form technical posts. Deep dives into CTI campaigns, malware families, vulnerability research, and security concepts I want to document properly.
  • CTFs Writeups from Capture The Flag competitions. Forensics, reversing, pwn, web, crypto — whatever I manage to solve. Sometimes writeups, sometimes just notes.
  • Hack The Box Walkthroughs for HTB machines and challenges. Step-by-step with technique notes, focused on understanding the path rather than just getting root.
  • Malware Notes Quick and detailed analysis notes. Behavior observations, YARA rules, IOCs, artifact walkthroughs, and CTI-adjacent notes on threat actors and campaigns.
  • Misc Anything that doesn't fit elsewhere. Tool notes, reading references, small scripts, random thoughts on security or geopolitics.
