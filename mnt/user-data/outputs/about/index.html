About

A little bit about who I am and what this place is.

// whoami

I go by m4gicxleep. I'm a junior security professional and student with a focus on Cyber Threat Intelligence, malware analysis, and hands-on labs. I work across both offensive and defensive areas — from understanding attacker tradecraft to building detection logic.

// what I work on

Most of my time goes into CTI research, studying malware behavior, and solving CTFs and Hack The Box machines. I'm also interested in YARA rules, artifact analysis, and geopolitical angles of cybersecurity. I write things down here so I don't forget them — and so others can use them.
